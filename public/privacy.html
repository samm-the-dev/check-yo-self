Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

What Check Yo Self Does

Check Yo Self is a personal budgeting app that reads your YNAB budget data to show you a daily spending number. It is a client-side Progressive Web App (PWA) with no backend server.

Data We Access

Check Yo Self connects to the YNAB API with read-only access. We request the minimum permissions needed: your budget categories, account balances, transactions, and scheduled transactions. We cannot create, modify, or delete any data in your YNAB account.

How Your Data Is Handled

  • Stored on your device only. All budget data is cached locally in your browser (IndexedDB) to reduce API calls. It is never sent to any server operated by us.
  • No backend, no analytics, no tracking. Check Yo Self runs entirely in your browser. There is no server-side component that receives, processes, or stores your data.
  • OAuth token. Your YNAB access token is stored in your browser's localStorage. It is only sent to YNAB's API servers (api.ynab.com) to fetch your budget data.
  • No third-party sharing. Your YNAB data is never shared with, sold to, or passed to any third party.

Data Retention

Cached data remains in your browser until you disconnect your YNAB account or clear your browser data. Disconnecting from the Settings page removes your access token, preferences, and all cached budget data immediately.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by disconnecting your account in Settings. This clears your access token, category tier preferences, and all cached YNAB data from your browser. You can also revoke Check Yo Self's access from your YNAB account settings. If you'd like to request confirmation that your data has been removed, contact us at the email below.

Security

Check Yo Self is served over HTTPS. Your YNAB credentials are never shared with this app — you authenticate directly on YNAB's website via OAuth. The access token is stored only in your browser and transmitted only to YNAB's API over encrypted connections.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, email privacy@samm-the.dev.
